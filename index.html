About Us

Grand Union Repair Café is a community-led initiative where people come together to connect, fix meaningful items, and reduce waste. As part of the Together Towards Zero movement, we're supporting a greener, more sustainable Brent by helping to keep useful items out of landfill.

Our Partners

Grand Union Repair Café is proud to work alongside the following partners who help make our community repairs possible.

London Community Kitchen

A grassroots organisation committed to tackling food insecurity and reducing waste. As Grand Union Community Hub operator, they aim to bring people together to build stronger, more connected communities.

Brent Council - Together Towards Zero

Through their Together Towards Zero Grant Fund, Brent Council are aiming to support communities in improving their local areas by making them greener, cleaner and more environmentally friendly.

Grand Union Development Trust

A community-led Social Enterprise and Charity established to strengthen community engagement in the Grand Union area.
